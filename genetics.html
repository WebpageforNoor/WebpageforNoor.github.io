My 21st century skill

The twenty first century skill I used the most during this process is number 3, Communication and collaboration.

In this project, I worked with a completly new team of people, at times, I can admit we did not collaborate well and did have mild disagreements and misunderstandings, however, we put that aside and worked with what we had, we each took on independent parts of the project, for example: I filmed and edited the start of the Interview video you saw, Nazih wrote the script and attempted to edit B-roll and Carter edited before interview and Credits. Overall, we put our diffrences aside, came up with a plan and completed the project.

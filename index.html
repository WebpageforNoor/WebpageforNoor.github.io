My experience:

Suprisingly, finding my theme was quite easy. I had two ideas in mind, Self harm and beauty. In the end, I decided with the latter.

In order to execute my idea, I used Photoshop in order to create a rough draft you saw above.

With my sketch in mind, it was time to take the actual photo: One of the simplest parts. I uploded the photo to my laptop, then to Photoshop, where I removed the color and any unneeded parts in order to get my final edit.

Once the editing was complete, It was time for the physical exicution.

We started by taking planks of woods, cutting it to an appropriate size. Once thats complete, It is time to pull a mesh screen across the frame. By far, one of my least favorite parts.

after what felt like hours of stapeling the mesh onto the wood, we printed our edited design onto Viynl.
